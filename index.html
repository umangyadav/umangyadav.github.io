Short Bio

I am currently in first year of Master of Applied Science Program in Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at University of Toronto working with Prof. Dimitrios Hatzinakos.

My thesis work is focused on Photoplethysmograph based Biometric System.

I received my Bachelor's Degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from SV National Institute of Technology - Surat, India in May, 2016.
